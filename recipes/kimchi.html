Kimchi Recipe


Ingredients

  • Napa cabbage
  • salt
  • sweet rice flour
  • sugar
  • water
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • onion
  • fish sauce
  • fermented squid
  • gochugaru
  • leek
  • carrot
  • Korean radish

Steps

  1. trim Napa cabbage
  2. Cut Napa cabbage to bite size slices
  3. Soak cabbage in cold water and add salt
  4. Let soak for 1.5 hrs and turn over every 30 minutes
  5. Rinse cabbage
  6. Drain Cabbage & set aside
  7. etc.
